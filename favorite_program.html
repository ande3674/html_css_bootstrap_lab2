Road Trip Guide

My program will be your best friend on your road trip. Enter your origin, your destination, and what type of recommendation you would like for your destination location, and the program will give you all of the information you need for your rest stops along the way. You will get the name of the city where you will stop, a suggestion on what to eat at that stop, and a recommendation for what to do once you arrive at your destination.
